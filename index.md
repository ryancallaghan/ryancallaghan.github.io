## About Me
---
 Hi, I'm Ryan! <br><br>
 I am an engineer passionate about medical devices, wearable devices,  biotechnology, and fitness technology. I am from and currently live in the New York City area. I have a bachelor's degree in mechanical engineering and a master's in biomedical engineering, both from Stevens Institute of Technology. I currently work as an electrophysiology clinical account specialist at Johnson & Johnson Medtech, where I assist electrophysiologists in diagnosing and treating cardiac arrhythmias. I have also worked as an R&D engineer at Johnson & Johnson's Medtech, where I led design change projects on existing products, created and verified a new test method, completed design verification and performance qualification reports, and facilitated change orders for material specification changes.  <br><br> I have experience with 3D computer-aided design, MATLAB, Python, Minitab, Tableau, Excel, and drafting technical reports. Outside of work, I am a very active person. I have been swimming since I was 5 years old, and I enjoy going skiing in the winter. I have also run in the NYC marathon, and plan to run again this year! 
  <br><br>This website serves to showcase some of the engineering projects I have completed during my academic career.
